Where did Parkoo come from??

As my kids have grown older, our time together becomes more and more important. My boys' desire to go to new places culminated with me no longer wanted to spend hours on Google map looking for a new park to visit helped guide me to this. A list of places we'd visited with the features offered at each location.
Now, with the technological science we have available to us, we want to offer our "research" to everybody else. It's important to get out and find something new and beautiful, to share with your loved ones, your pets or even your inner child.
While this app is in no way perfect, I hope it at least helps you and those who matter most to you find somewhere new to play, to fish, or to just get outside.
